## Selected Approach: Neo-Constructivism

I am selecting the **Neo-Constructivism** approach for this website. This design philosophy aligns with the goal of creating a thoughtful, distinctive presence that stands out from typical resume or portfolio sites. The dynamic diagonal compositions and bold geometric elements will create visual interest while maintaining clarity and readability. The warm color palette (cream, navy, burnt orange, teal) conveys confidence and approachability, which is appropriate for a professional website that focuses on thinking and philosophy.

The diagonal sections and zigzag layout will naturally guide the reader through Varun's thinking, creating a narrative flow. The bold typography and geometric accents will emphasize key ideas without overwhelming the content. This approach avoids the generic centered layouts and purple gradients that are common in AI-generated designs, while still maintaining a professional and credible tone.
